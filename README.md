# 🌍 Carbon Credit Marketplace (Soroban Smart Contract)

## 📌 Project Description

The Carbon Credit Marketplace is a decentralized platform built on the Stellar Soroban smart contract framework. It enables organizations and individuals to trade carbon credits transparently and securely.

Carbon credits represent the reduction of greenhouse gas emissions. This marketplace allows verified credits to be listed, bought, and transferred without intermediaries.

---

## ⚙️ What it does

- Allows users to list carbon credits for sale
- Enables buyers to purchase carbon credits
- Maintains ownership records on-chain
- Provides transparent and immutable tracking of credits

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🌱 Decentralized carbon credit listing
- 💰 Simple marketplace for buying credits
- 🔐 Ownership tracking on blockchain
- ⚡ Fast and low-cost transactions using Stellar
- 📊 Transparent and tamper-proof records

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- Soroban Smart Contracts (Rust)
- Stellar Blockchain

---

## 📦 Functions

### 1. list_credit
List a new carbon credit for sale.

**Parameters:**
- `credit_id`: Unique ID of the carbon credit
- `owner`: Address of the seller
- `price`: Price of the credit

---

### 2. buy_credit
Buy an existing carbon credit.

**Parameters:**
- `credit_id`: ID of the credit
- `buyer`: Address of the buyer

---

### 3. get_credit
Retrieve details of a carbon credit.
